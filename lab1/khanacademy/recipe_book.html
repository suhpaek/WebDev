Pancakes

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: hz
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 500ml
Eggs 2
Flour 200g
Butter 30g
Sugar 30g
Salt 2g

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with sugar and salt.
  2. Add milk and mix well.
  3. Slowly add flour and mix until the batter is smooth.
  4. Add vegetable oil and mix again.
  5. Heat a frying pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  6. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and move the pan to spread it evenly.
  7. Fry the pancake for about 1â€“2 minutes on each side until golden.
  8. Repeat with the rest of the batter.

Tiramisu

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: hz
Ingredients Quantity
Savoyardi cookies 250g
Eggs 6
Espresso 300ml
Cacao 30g
Sugar 150g

Instructions:

  1. Separate the egg yolks and egg whites.
  2. In a bowl, beat the egg yolks with sugar until creamy.
  3. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  4. In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff.
  5. Gently fold the egg whites into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Dip ladyfinger cookies quickly into coffee.
  7. Place a layer of soaked ladyfingers in a dish.
  8. Spread a layer of cream mixture on top.
  9. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  10. Cover the top with cocoa powder.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
