Travel to Sanya

My feelings

Last summer, I traveled to China and visited the city of Sanya. It was my first time there, and I felt both excited and a little nervous. The weather was very hot and humid, but the beaches were beautiful and relaxing.

I really enjoyed walking near the sea and trying new food, even though some dishes were unusual for me. Sometimes it was hard to communicate because of the language, but people were generally friendly.

Overall, the trip was a good experience, and it helped me learn more about a different culture and see a new part of the world.

About city

Sanya is a popular resort city in the south of China, located on Hainan Island. It is famous for its warm climate, beautiful beaches, and clear blue sea. Many tourists visit Sanya to relax, swim, and enjoy the tropical nature.

The city has many modern hotels, shopping centers, and restaurants. You can also find traditional Chinese markets and local seafood places. The atmosphere in Sanya is calm and comfortable, which makes it a great place for vacation.
